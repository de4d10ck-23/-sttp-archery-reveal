Our Archery Classes

Choose from our beginner, intermediate, and advanced programs designed for students of all skill levels.

Find the Right Class for You

Straight to the Point Archery offers structured training programs for beginners, developing archers, and advanced students who want to improve their performance. Each class is designed to build skills step by step in a safe, supportive, and focused environment.

Whether you are learning the basics for the first time or refining your shooting technique, our classes help you grow in confidence, discipline, and accuracy.

A101 Basic Archery

Introduction to archery for beginners with a strong focus on range safety, proper form, shooting technique, and basic equipment use.

  • Schedule: Weekends
  • Cost: $120
  • Prerequisite: None

A102 Intermediate Archery

Designed for students who already understand the fundamentals and want to improve their shooting skills, consistency, control, and overall accuracy.

  • Schedule: Saturdays
  • Cost: $150
  • Prerequisite: A101 Basic Archery

A103 Advanced Archery

Advanced-level instruction for archers who want to strengthen precision, mental focus, performance, and competitive readiness through more challenging practice.

  • Schedule: Sundays
  • Cost: $180
  • Prerequisite: A102 Intermediate Archery
