About Straight to the Point Archery

Building skills, confidence, and community through archery since 2005.

Who We Are

Straight to the Point Archery is a professional archery training center dedicated to helping students develop confidence, focus, discipline, and skill. Since 2005, we have provided a safe and supportive environment where beginners and experienced archers can learn and grow.

Our programs are designed for all ages and skill levels. Whether you are trying archery for the first time or improving your competitive technique, our team is here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide safe, high-quality archery instruction for students of all ages and skill levels. We are committed to building confidence, discipline, and focus through the sport of archery.

Our History

Founded in 2005, Straight to the Point Archery has proudly served the Tacoma community for many years. What started as a small training space has grown into a trusted archery education center known for excellence and safety.

Our Facilities

Our facility features modern equipment, a safe indoor range, and a welcoming environment for every student. We maintain our equipment carefully to ensure the best possible learning experience.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing an inclusive, respectful, and encouraging atmosphere. We welcome students from all backgrounds and believe everyone deserves the opportunity to learn and enjoy archery.
